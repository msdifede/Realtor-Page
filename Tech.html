Robotic Leagues

FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC). (Grades 9-12)

FRC is an international high school robotics competition. Each year, teams of high school students, coaches, and mentors work during a six-week period to build game-playing robots that weigh up to 125 pounds. Mineola's team has been to the World Championships in Detroit in 2018 & 2019.
